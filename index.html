Cat Sound Categories to Imitate

 

I am doing research on identification of cat sounds and for that, I need to record human vocal imitations of cat sounds.
Here, you can find a set of example Cat Sounds divided by 3 subcategories as Meow, Purr, Growling which are downloaded from Freesound and combined.

You have to listen to the audio clips with several examples of a particular type of cat sound, and then record yourself making 10 imitations for each category of these sounds, leaving some silence between imitations as in the examples. You should record the imitations in a silent environment.

After recording your imitations please fill the form on the right and send your recordings to me by email. Your email should consist of 3 sound files as meow, purr, and growling.

 

ceren.can01@estudiant.upf.edu

 

Thanks for participating and Happy Meowing!

 

Meow

The classic tonal communication sound made by cats.

Purr

A tonal fluttering sound made by cats to indicate contentment or relaxed pleasure.

Growling

A low, guttural vocalization produced by animals as a warning, a sign of aggression, or to express anger.
