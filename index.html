Cat Sound Categories to Imitate

The aim of this research is distinguishing the sounds of domestic cats from the sounds of domestic cats imitated by humans.
Here you can find a set of Cat Sounds divided by 3 categories which are downloaded from FreeSound.org.
FreeSound is a platform for the collaborative creation of open audio collections labeled by humans and based on Freesound content.
To create a data set consisting of the vocalizations of the imitation cat sounds, here reference sounds were created.
You should listen to the combination of various clips for each category to be able to create imitations.
Each category approximately 20-30 seconds long and belongs to a different source.
You need to record 10 imitations for each cat subcategory. Duration of the imitations may vary and will be shortened if necessary.

Meow

The classic tonal communication sound made by cats.

Purr

A tonal fluttering sound made by cats to indicate contentment or relaxed pleasure.

Growling

A low, guttural vocalization produced by animals as a warning, a sign of aggression, or to express anger.
